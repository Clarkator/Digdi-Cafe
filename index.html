Our Story

From Bean to Cup

Founded in 2015, Digdi Cafe began as a small passion project between two coffee enthusiasts. What started as a humble cart serving pour-overs has grown into a beloved community hub where coffee is more than a drinkâ€”it's an experience.

We travel directly to coffee-growing regions to build relationships with farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Every bean tells a story, and we're honored to bring those stories to your cup.

Our baristas are trained for months to master the art of extraction, ensuring each beverage meets our exacting standards. Whether you're a coffee connoisseur or new to specialty coffee, we'll guide you to your perfect cup.
